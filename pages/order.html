How to Order

Takeout
  1. Browse our online menu and select your desired items.
  2. Customize your order with any special requests or modifications.
  3. Proceed to checkout and provide your contact information.
  4. Choose your preferred pickup time.
  5. Complete your payment securely online.
  6. Pick up your order at the designated time and enjoy!
Delivery
  1. Explore our menu and add your favorite dishes to your cart.
  2. Specify any special instructions or dietary preferences.
  3. Enter your delivery address and contact details.
  4. Select your preferred delivery time or choose 'ASAP'.
  5. Choose your payment method and complete the transaction.
  6. Track your order in real-time and receive your delicious meal at your doorstep.
